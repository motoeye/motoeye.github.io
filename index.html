BACKGROUND

Transport Canada claims that the number of yearly vehicular accidents have been downtrending but motorcycle accidents, a subset of vehicular accidents, have been steadily increasing. Considering motorcyclists generally ride for 4-6 months of the year, the rate of accidents is concerning. Motorcycle registrations compromise of only 1.4% of the total vehicle registrations, yet motorcycle accidents make up 13.9% of all vehicular accidents in Canada.

Stakeholder surveys have indicated that 36.6% of accidents or near-misses were related to obstacles in front of the motorcycle with over 80% of obstacle related collisions involving animals and road debris in front of the motorcycle

Evidently, there is an urgent need to help improve motorcycle safety on Canadian roads

PRODUCT - MotoEye

Forward Facing Obstacle Detection & Notification Device for Motorcyclists

MotoEye Mounted to a Motorcycle
3D Model View - Forward Facing
3D Model View - Rider Facing

Device Features/Functionality :

  • Obstacle Detection for Relative Speeds between 10 to 50 Km/Hr
  • Obstacle Detection for Hazards up to 30m Away
  • System Status Indicators - Green LED
  • Hazard Warning Indicators - Red LED
  • Utilizes Deep Learning for Obstacle Detection
  • Active Vibrational Dampening
  • Splash-Proof Acrylic Housing
  • Modular & Compatible Wide Range Motorcycles
