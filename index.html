BACKGROUND

Transport Canada claims that the number yearly vehicular accidents have been downtrending but motorcycle accidents, a subset of vehicular accidents, have been steadily increasing. Considering motorcyclists generally ride 4-6 months of the year, the rate of accidents is concerning. Motorcycle registrations comproise of only 1.4% of the total vehicle registrations, yet motorcycle accidents make up 13.9% of all vehicular accidents in Canada.

Evidently, there is an urgent need to help improve motorcycle safety on the Canadian roads

PROPOSED SOLUTION

Stakeholder survey have indicated that 36.6% of accidents or near-misses were related to obstacles in front of the motorcycle with over 80% of obstacle related collisions involved animals and road debris in front of the motorcycle

Forward Facing Obstacle Detection & Notification Device for Motorcyclists

Final chosen design with interim changes applied
Final 3D CAD model

Device Features/Functionality :

  • Warning Detection for speeds of up to 50 Km/Hr
  • System Status Indicators
  • Utilizes Cutting Edge Deep Learning Techonology for obstacle detection
  • Active Vibrational Dampening
  • Waterproof and Weather resistant
  • Modular & Compantiple wide range motorcycle
